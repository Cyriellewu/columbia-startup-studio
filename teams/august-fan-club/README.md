## Submission Overview

This folder contains the Week 6 submission for Evidence of Life: style guide, live landing page link, demand test plan, MVP feature list, and Google Analytics certification status.

Evidence of Life is a personal life OS that turns scattered plans into visible proof of what actually happened. It is not just a journaling app or another todo app. The core loop is:

> plan → action → evidence → recap

Users can plan tasks on a timeline, start focus sessions, save unfinished work as evidence, add photos and locations, and later review the day through recap, calendar, and map views.

## Demand Test Summary

We will test two message angles:

1. **Your day did not disappear** — focused on ADHD-style time blindness and emotional relief.
2. **Stop ending the day with a guilty todo list** — focused on Done List behavior and making unfinished work count.

Budget: approximately $200 over 5 days, split evenly across both angles.

Success threshold: at least 5% landing page signup conversion on one angle, or at least 40 waitlist signups total, with cost per signup at or below $5.

## MVP Summary

The v1 product focuses on five features:

1. Daily Plan + Timeline
2. Focus Timer + Actual Time Tracking
3. Done List / Daily Recap
4. Moment Capture with Photos and Locations
5. Calendar + Map Review

These support the core transformation from intention to evidence: a task starts as a plan, becomes a focus session, and then becomes part of the daily recap.
